SGS Academy Can Now Deliver All IRCA Approved Courses through Virtual Instructor Led Training

sgs news 0 Comment


SGS is pleased to announce that it has received approval from the International Register of Certificated Auditors (IRCA) to deliver all IRCA certified courses through the Virtual Instructor Led Training (VILT) format.

The IRCA is the world's first and largest international certification body for auditors of management systems and is a division of the Chartered Quality Institute (CQI).

VILT is the middle ground between classroom based training and eLearning – training is scheduled as though for a classroom but is delivered remotely and hosted by a live instructor via a conferencing application. Learners get the benefit of collaboration with others while avoiding issues associated with travel, such as safety and costs.
